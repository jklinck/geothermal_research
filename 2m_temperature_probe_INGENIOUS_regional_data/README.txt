Shapefile (NAD83 Geographic) and Excel spreadsheet containing shallow 2-meter temperature probe data.

Attributes:

Columns A-D: Area, County, and State are are for the group of survey points, and where a survey crossed borders the counties are for the entire group and not individual points, e.g. Mineral-Nye indicates that the survey crosses between Mineral and Nye counties and some points are in Mineral and some are in Nye county.
Columns E-F: East and North NAD83 zone 11N UTM coordinates.
Columns G-I: Temperature measurements. All locations have temperature measurement for 2m (T2m), and when available temperatures at 1m and 1.5m T1m and T15m respectively.  
Column J: 2mDAB 2m temperature normalized to average background calculated for the specific area.
Column K: StCode: B = Base station, LT = Long term station
Column L: Original station number where recorded
Column M: Date of original temperature measurement for columns G-I, or date seasonal correction was made to, if data was seasonally corrected and initial temperature and date are not available.
Column N: Group measurements were conducted by. GPO, Navy Geothermal Program Office; GBCGE Great Basin Center for Geothermal Energy, Nevada Bureau of Mines and Geology, University of Nevada, Reno; GTP Geothermal Technical Partners Inc.
Columns O-AK: Additional measurements (base station and long term measurement data) are designated as T1m-N, T15m-N, T2m-N and Date-N, where N is the additional measurement number. 

Additional attribute metadata including region code descriptions can be found in Excel spreadsheet.
